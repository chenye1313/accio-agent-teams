## 2. HOTBLIZZARD 样例

```yaml
differentiation_analysis:
  role_id: "05_Differentiation差异化定位师"
  input_profile_id: "case2_hotblizzard"
  status: "pass"

  positioning_statement:
    cn: "为正在测款的街头潮牌主，提供从小单验证到柔性量产的新品供应链伙伴。"
    en: "A new-product supply chain partner for streetwear startups, from small-batch validation to flexible production."
    path_type: "hybrid"
    one_sentence_logic: "它既要抢占 small-batch validation 这个任务词，也要用 7 天打样、柔性量产、认证和合作品牌前置信任。"

  differentiation_points:
    - point: "小单测试到量产补货的衔接能力"
      linked_job: "低风险验证新品并进入稳定补货"
      linked_capability: "小单测试、柔性量产、7 天打样 + 45 天量产"
      proof_asset: "case2 delivery_cycle / main_order_type"
    - point: "多维定制支撑潮牌风格一致性"
      linked_job: "把设计概念变成可售样衣"
      linked_capability: "面料、工艺、logo、标签、辅料定制"
      proof_asset: "case2 customization_services"
    - point: "合规与大牌合作降低供应商风险"
      linked_job: "降低采购和品质风险"
      linked_capability: "Oeko-Tex / CP65 / New Balance 等合作"
      proof_asset: "case2 certifications / partnerships"

  forbidden_claims:
    - claim: "爆款制造专家"
      reason: "缺少爆款数据或测款案例。"
    - claim: "全链路品牌孵化专家"
      reason: "品牌孵化需要完整客户成长案例支撑。"

  not_for:
    - buyer: "只找最低价现货的大批发商"
      reason: "该定位服务的是需要测款、定制和柔性补货的品牌主。"

  expression_path:
    first_screen_claim: "Start small, scale with confidence."
    proof_assets_to_show:
      - "7-day sampling"
      - "45-day bulk production"
      - "custom fabrics / trims / labels"
      - "certifications and brand partnerships"
```
